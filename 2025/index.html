Call for Proposals

We are inviting speakers from the community to participate in TinyGo Conference 2025. This is a fantastic opportunity to share your knowledge and experiences related to TinyGo. Diverse topics and perspectives are welcome.

Categories

  • Fundamentals: Topics for beginners related to TinyGo
  • Applications: Case studies and real-world projects
  • Tools and Techniques: Methods to enhance development efficiency
  • IoT and Embedded Systems: Developing IoT and embedded systems with TinyGo
  • Community and Education: How TinyGo is utilized in educational settings

Deadlines

  • Submission Deadline: August 31, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: September 15, 2025

Submit proposals through the form on the official website. For any questions, please contact us at email@example.com.
